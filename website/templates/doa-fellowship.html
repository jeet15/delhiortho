DOA Fellowship

Delhi Orthopaedic Association grants three travelling fellowships for life members of the DOA, less than 30 yrs, 30 to 40 yrs, above 40 years of age. The selected candidates are given Rs. 10,000 each towards this fellowship. We invite the applications for Delhi Orthopaedic Association Travelling Fellowships every year. The criteria for selection are very objective and as follows:
