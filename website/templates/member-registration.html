Member Registration

Life Membership:Orthopedic Surgeon with proper training in the specialty with D Ortho.,MS (Ortho.), Mch (Ortho.), DNB (Ortho.), FRCS (Ortho.) qualifications are eligible for life membership .

Please send a photocopy of the qualification along with a Draft or money transfer of Rs 3000/- to ” Delhi Orthopedics Association”. You can submit the form online with or without bank transfer. Else dispatch to the secretary.
